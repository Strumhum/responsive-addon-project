Why

Why is Ted Lasso my favourite TV series?

Jason Sudeikis is Ted Lasso, a football coach hired to manage a British soccer team. What he lacks in knowledge, he makes up for with optimism, determination...and biscuits.

What makes Ted Lasso so successful, popular and fun to watch is its 'feel-good' atmosphere. The series just makes you feel better, it uplifts you and it certainly makes you look at everything in a more positive and optimistic way. Joyful and funny moments are delicately balanced against heavier emotional storylines. You fall in love with the cast of wonderful characters and the unbridled empathetic coaching style of Lasso. I BELIEVE in Ted Lasso!
